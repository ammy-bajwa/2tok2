    What are the recommended gas prices?

    Default gas price is generally works well, but occasionally it makes sense to adjust it to your needs. Raise it to speed up your transaction or lower the price to save some ETH. In these cases automatic setting helps you to find the right gas price for your transaction.

    • Safe low - This is a price that is both cheap and successful. With this setting it may take a bit longer to get your transaction mined, but it is safe to use and should be confirmed propmpty.
    • Standard - This price is fine for most of the miners accounting for at least 50% of the blocks mined.
    • Fastest - The lowest gas price accepted by all top miners, so transactions on this setting should be accepted by the top mining pools.
    Speed SafeLow (< 30m) Standard (< 5m) Fast (< 2m)

    Gas Price (fiat) {safeLowNowFiat} {standardNowFiat} {fastNowFiat}
